Security
Your data is
yours. Always.

Bank-level security isn't a feature — it's the foundation. We designed askwijs so you can trust it with your most sensitive financial data.

🔒

PSD2 Compliant

Read-only bank access by default. No credentials stored — OAuth tokens only. Banks are legally required to support this standard.

🇪🇺

EU Data Storage

All data stored in Netherlands/Germany. GDPR compliant by design. Your financial data never leaves the EU.

🛡️

2FA on All Actions

Two-factor authentication required for any filing action. Every agent action logged with a full audit trail you can review anytime.

👁️

Full Transparency

See exactly what Wijs did and why. Every categorization, every calculation, every filing — explained in plain language.

⚡

You Stay in Control

Wijs never files without your approval. Disconnect your bank at any time. Export all your data whenever you want.

🔐

SOC 2 Type II (2026)

Enterprise-grade security certification in progress. Built to the same standard as the tools your bank uses.
