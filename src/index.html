Connecting your finances requires absolute trust. askwijs is built to the same security standards as the banks you already use.

🔒

Bank-level encryption

All data encrypted at rest and in transit with AES-256. Your bank credentials never touch our servers — we use tokenised PSD2 connections only.

🏦

PSD2 Open Banking

We connect to your bank through licensed PSD2 infrastructure — the same regulated standard used by Revolut, Wise, and every major EU fintech.

📋

AVG / GDPR compliant

Your data is stored in the EU, never sold, never shared with third parties. Export or delete everything at any time.

Read-only access

We only read your transaction data — we can never initiate payments or move money. You stay in full control.
