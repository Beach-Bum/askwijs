How it works
From bank account
to filed return.

Four steps. Most of them happen automatically while you focus on your work.

Connect your bank

Securely link ING, ABN AMRO, Rabobank, or Bunq via PSD2 Open Banking. Takes 60 seconds.

2

Wijs categorizes everything

Every transaction — past 90 days and going forward — is automatically sorted: business, personal, deductible.
