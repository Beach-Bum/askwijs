Security & trust
Bank-level security.
Zero compromises.

You're trusting us with your financial data. We take that seriously — here's exactly how we protect it.

🔒

PSD2 Compliant

Bank connections through regulated Open Banking. The same standard used by every major European bank.

👁️

Read-only access

Wijs can see your transactions. It cannot move money, make payments, or change anything in your bank account. Ever.

🔐

AES-256 encrypted

All data encrypted at rest with AES-256 and in transit with TLS 1.3. Your data is unreadable without your keys.

🇪🇺

GDPR compliant

Your data stays in the EU. You own it entirely — export or delete everything at any time, instantly.

🚫

No data sold. Ever.

Our only revenue is your subscription. We never sell, share, or monetize your financial data. Full stop.

✅

DigiD integration

Filing uses the same authentication as the Belastingdienst itself — so the government trusts exactly who is submitting.
