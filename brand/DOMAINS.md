## Name Rationale: askwijs

**Why it works:**
- "Ask wise" in English — oracle/advisor positioning built in
- **wijs** = wise in Dutch — native speakers get it immediately
- `ask` prefix creates a conversational, AI-native format
- Short, memorable, unique — no conflicts found
- Works bilingually across NL and EN markets
- "Vraag het aan Wijs" — natural Dutch phrasing for marketing

**Why not the others:**
- `askotto.ai` — taken, though `askotto.nl` is available
- `askboek.ai` — available but less warm/human
- `askfisc.ai` — available but feels corporate
- `BTWijs` — clever but harder to say and type
